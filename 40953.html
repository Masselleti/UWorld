HIV regulatory & accessory proteins

Tat

Promotes transcription of viral genes

Rev

Enhances export of unspliced viral transcripts from nucleus to cytoplasm

Nef

Downregulates host CD4 & MHC I proteins (inhibits immune recognition)

Vpu

Aids viral assembly & release

Vpr

Facilitates nuclear entry & inhibits host cell division

Vif

Increases infectivity & viral yield

MHC = major histocompatibility complex.
