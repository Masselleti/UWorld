Cytokine

Major source

Major effects

IL-1

Macrophages
  • ↑ Neutrophil & macrophage migration
  • ↑ Acute-phase reactants, fever & shock

IL-2

T cells
  • ↑ T-cell activation & proliferation
  • ↑ NK cell & macrophage activity
  • ↑ B-cell growth

IL-3

T cells
  • ↑ Hematopoiesis

IL-4

TH2 cells
  • ↑ TH2-cell differentiation
  • ↑ B-cell growth
  • ↑ Isotype switching to IgE

IL-5

TH2 cells
  • ↑ Differentiation of eosinophils
  • ↑ Isotype switching to IgA

IL-6

Macrophages
  • ↑ T- & B-cell growth
  • ↑ Osteoclast activity
  • ↑ Acute-phase reactants & fever

IL-8

Macrophages
T cells
  • ↑ Neutrophil activation & chemotaxis

IL-10

TH2 cells
  • ↓ TH1-cell differentiation
  • ↓ Cell-mediated immunity & APC activity
  • ↑ B-cell function

IL-12

Macrophages
  • ↑ TH1-cell differentiation
  • ↑ NK-cell & CD8+ T-cell activity

IFN-gamma

TH1 cells
NK cells
  • ↑ Intracellular killing by macrophages
  • ↑ MHC class I & II expression
  • ↑ TH1-cell differentiation

TGF-beta

Most cell types
  • ↓ Immune cell function
  • ↑ Matrix synthesis & angiogenesis

GM-CSF

T cells
Macrophages
  • ↑ Growth & differentiation of myeloid progenitors

TNF-alpha

Macrophages
  • ↑ Inflammation & cell-mediated immunity
  • ↑ Acute-phase reactants, fever & shock

APC = antigen-presenting cell; GM-CSF = granulocyte-macrophage colony-stimulating factor; IFN = interferon; MHC = major histocompatibility complex; NK = natural killer;
TGF = tumor growth factor; TNF = tumor necrosis factor.
